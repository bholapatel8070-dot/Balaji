✦ Our Legacy

A Heritage of Luxury

Nestled in the heart of Bhawani Mandi, Madhya Pradesh, Shri Balaji Resort is the crown jewel of hospitality in the region. For over two decades, we have been welcoming guests with open arms, offering a seamless blend of royal Indian tradition and contemporary elegance.

Every corner of our resort tells a story — from the hand-crafted décor that celebrates Madhya Pradesh's rich culture to the lush gardens and pristine facilities that ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.
